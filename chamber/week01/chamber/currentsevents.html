Current Events

Zongo Adventure Festival

Experience the thrill of the Zongo Adventure Festival, a vibrant celebration of outdoor activities and adrenaline-pumping adventures. Join us from July 15th to July 24th for a week filled with exciting events, including hiking, rock climbing, zip-lining, and more. Get ready to unleash your inner adventurer and create unforgettable memories.

River Rafting Expedition

Calling all thrill-seekers! Embark on an unforgettable river rafting expedition on the Zongo River. From August 5th to August 10th, join our experienced guides as they navigate the thrilling rapids and provide you with an adrenaline-fueled adventure like no other. Don't miss this opportunity to conquer the mighty Zongo River.

Zongo Music Festival

Get ready for the Zongo Music Festival, a celebration of rhythm, melody, and cultural diversity. From September 16th to September 18th, immerse yourself in the vibrant sounds of local and international musicians as they showcase their talents across multiple stages. Dance, sing, and revel in the lively atmosphere of this musical extravaganza.

Zongo Food Fair

Indulge your taste buds at the Zongo Food Fair, a culinary delight showcasing the rich flavors and traditional cuisines of the region. From October 10th to October 15th, sample an array of mouthwatering dishes prepared by talented local chefs. Experience the gastronomic wonders of Zongo and satisfy your cravings for exquisite food.
